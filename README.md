## 📋 Quality Standards (The Senior Framework)

### [cite_start]1. Defect Lifecycle Management [cite: 26]
[cite_start]We proactively report, monitor, and validate bugs[cite: 34]. A "Definition of Done" report must include:
* [cite_start]Clear, reproducible steps[cite: 22].
* [cite_start]Technical evidence (Logs, Console errors, Screenshots)[cite: 22].
* Business impact analysis.

### 2. Data Integrity (SQL for QA)
We validate the "truth" behind the UI. Our team uses SQL to:
* Ensure backend data persistence.
* Verify complex business logic and edge cases.
* Prepare controlled data sets for functional execution.

### 3. Accessibility & Compliance
[cite_start]We actively conduct accessibility testing to ensure applications meet global compliance guidelines[cite: 25].
